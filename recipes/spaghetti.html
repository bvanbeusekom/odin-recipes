Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked spaghetti (about 1.5 to 2 pounds)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can condensed cream of vegetable soup

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and cook, stirring frequently, until softened but not browned. Add the garlic and cook, stirring until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the tomatoes, soup, and chicken soup to the skillet, stirring to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Add the vegetable soup and cook, stirring to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add the Parmesan cheese and cook, stirring until melted and bubbly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
