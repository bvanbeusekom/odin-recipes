Pizza

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley leaves
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the sugar and egg until smooth.
  3. Add the flour, salt, and vegetable oil, and mix until well combined.
  4. Add the chopped onions, garlic, crushed tomatoes, basil, parsley, and Parmesan cheese. Mix until well combined.
  5. Add the mozzarella cheese, and mix until well combined.
  6. Pour the dough into prepared baking dishes, and shape them into round pizzas.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the cheese is melted.
